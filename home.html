Program We Offer

EduKick Football Academy offers two programs.

Train Young

This program is designed for youngsters in the range of 8-16 years. With football the education is also important as such the academy house a school and hostel where the students can concentrate on schooling, stay on campus and then provide enough time learn and play football also.

BE PRO
